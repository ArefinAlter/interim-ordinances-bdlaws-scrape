Negotiable Instruments Act, 1881
(Act No. XXVI of 1881) এর section 141 এর clause (c) এর পরিবর্তে নিম্নরূপ clause (c) প্রতিস্থাপিত হইবে, যথা:-
‘‘(c) no court inferior to that of a court of Metropoliton Joint Sessions Judge or of a court of Joint Sessions Judge, as the case may be, shall try any offence punishable under section 138 where the face value of the cheque exceeds Tk. 5 (five) lac; and every other offence punishable under section 138 shall be triable by a court of Metropolitan Magistrate or Magistrate of the first class.’’।
